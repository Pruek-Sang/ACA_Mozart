# Valid Room Templates

> Source: MCP Core template definitions  
> Purpose: Validation source for room template selection

## Living Rooms
- `ROOMT-LIVING-STD` - Standard living room (<25m²)
- `ROOMT-LIVING-LARGE` - Large living room (≥25m²)

## Bedrooms
- `ROOMT-BEDROOM-STD` - Standard bedroom
- `ROOMT-BEDROOM-MASTER` - Master bedroom with ensuite

## Kitchens
- `ROOMT-KITCHEN-STD` - Standard kitchen
- `ROOMT-KITCHEN-HEAVY` - Heavy load kitchen (>5kW appliances, induction cookers)

## Bathrooms
- `ROOMT-BATHROOM-STD` - Standard bathroom
- `ROOMT-BATHROOM-MASTER` - Master bathroom with water heater

## Dining Rooms
- `ROOMT-DINING-STD` - Standard dining room

## Utility/Storage
- `ROOMT-STORAGE-STD` - Storage room
- `ROOMT-LAUNDRY-STD` - Laundry room

## Others
- `ROOMT-GARAGE-STD` - Garage
- `ROOMT-OFFICE-STD` - Home office
- `ROOMT-BALCONY-STD` - Balcony
- `ROOMT-EXTERIOR-STD` - Exterior area (front yard, garden, parking)
- `ROOMT-CORRIDOR-STD` - Corridor/hallway
